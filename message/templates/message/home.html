About

Us

The MADUTCH GROUP originated within the construction sector specializing in general construction, road markings, building maintenance, erection of sports facilities, plumbing, panting, paving, tiling, electrical work, fencing, burglar proofing, residential renovations. remodeling and erection of homes, offices in the era of modern designs.

Madutch Property Development subsidiary has the ability to provide market leading industry knowledge, experience and expertise in the construction sector.

The business later added subsidiary companies to the profile transformed it from what started as purely a construction company to a group of companies in aid to create jobs as well as create a one stop consortium for its clients.

The additional services expands The MADUTCH GROUP to specialize in General Supplies (General Home & Office Supplies), Consulting, Manufacturing (Households detergents), Cleaning and Upholstery and Landscaping.
